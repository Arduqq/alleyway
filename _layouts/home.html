Euphoria

Euphoria is a prevalent feeling that we all have felt in different contexts of our lives. Make your pets shine, burn them up, make them rise again. What does euphoria mean to you? How is it part of our society? What food is it that just makes you full of joy? Does euphoria and dysphoria shape identity and is their interplay a desire we all have? What gives you the drive to love and embrace and what makes us eager to learn from our experiences?

Where am I?

"Aah, a fellow tax payer, come come, I will show you around! My name is Ratterdam, the Vengeful! I might not look it, but my offerings are some of the *chef's kiss* most splendid ones! Would you like to take a look at our latest fruits of labour?"

What's an offering?

"That's how we call our precious little pets that we care for so lovingly. The Rat King will announce a theme for every month in which we venture the world of Subeta, caring for those creatures that fit their fantasy the best. Do not worry! We won't harm your precious friend. The rat king is not barbaric after all. They are curious of what those creative minds will come up with. We celebrate every pet the best we can, no worries, we do it for love!"

How do I participate?

"Well, nothing easier than that! If you manage to care for a pet that would fit under the monthly theme, send us a message through the Can Phone! We will take a quick look and put it riiiiight here!"

Why should I participate?

"Oh how I fear such questions. Just take a look around. This allyway is quite the unremarkable place, wouldn't you say so? The Rat King thrives on creativity - their people are fueled by their care for their pets. We don't want your little treasure to get lost in this chaos - we want to acknowledge your efforts and maybe even give something back! Not only will we award you with a shiny little trinket to decorate your pet with, but we will arrange a small form of compensation for your service. It is nothing big, but we hope that you see our humble gesture as nothing but admiration."
